Start of Skateboarding

So, my journey started when I was 10. So, I used to always watch kids play with their skate boards around the parks everywhere, so as time passed on, I started to encourage myself to skate board like others!

So, when I got my first skate board, I didn’t know how to use it, and didn’t even know how to ride it. I kept failing and failing at things that others could do, until after some months of practicing, I became a pretty good stake-boarder. So, when I started to skate board, then I started going with my friends to places I never have been in. So, after like 2 hours being with my friends when I was skate boarding one day, I came across this skate board park.

It was pretty big, and had the smoothest ground, any skate boarder would want. And from then on, almost every day I kept going to that place with and without my friends. Basically, that was a very awesome skate board park I’ve went to.

I will probably not find anything similar or better than that only skate park!
